Mapping Medieval Galician-Portuguese Literature and its Networks

This project was completed for LING 1050: Computational Methods in the Humanities, taught by David Birnbaum. In it, we explore bibliographical data about lyrical poets in the middle ages and then map it using social media network programs. You can find out more by reading the About section. We invite you to explore our project and enjoy!
