Mapping Medieval Galician-Portuguese Literature and its Networks

This project* was completed for LING 1050: Computational Methods in the Humanities, taught by David Birnbaum. In it, we explore bibliographical data about lyrical poets in the Middle Ages and then map it using both social network analysis and geographical information programs. You can find out more by reading the About and Research sections. We invite you to explore our project and enjoy!
