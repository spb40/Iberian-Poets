Mapping Medieval Galician-Portuguese Literature and its Networks

For the mapping part of our project, we used both Google Earth and Google Maps, and also social network graphing.

In the Google Maps section you can find the different maps that depict our poets' journeys divided into five different periods.

If you have downloaded the Google Earth plugin (or if you do not mind doing so now), please check out our Google Earth globe, which includes a timeline that allows you to see how these journeys occurred within a time reference.

If you prefer to highlight the relationships themselves, disregarding the geographical references, you may find our Social Network graph more appealing.
